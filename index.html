About Me

I’m Zolile Zoko, born and bred in a small township in the Free State called Botshabelo, which means "Place of Refuge" when translated from Sesotho to English. Every day, I push myself to become a better engineer by giving my all and staying open to learning. I want to use the skills I’ve gained at UCT to make a real difference in society, in any way I can. I love getting lost in a problem, wrestling with it, exploring countless solutions, and then realizing that sometimes the simplest approach is the best first step. I learn fast, work well with others, and care deeply about making technology useful for real people.
